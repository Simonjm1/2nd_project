What Makes a Chair, a Chair?

The definition of a chair is 'a seperate seat for one person'. Which means that anything made to be used for one person's bottom, can be considered a chair.

The Most Common Chairs

The most widely used chair are the ones with a back and four legs. chairs often typically are cylinder-like in shape without legs, and almost always with support for the arms.
